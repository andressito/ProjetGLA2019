• Who We Are


    ChuChuFly is a free platform that allows you to share flights in the least complicated way possible.
    Since your safety is one of our most prominent goals, all our pilots are certified and have valid licences.
    You need a one way flight less expensive than the aircompanies market,
    or maybe you're just in the mood for an airtrip, perhaps a visit to the sky?
    Just by a simple click, you can book your seat as possibly single as for a group.
    Because our team puts you first, ChuchuFly is the future of flight sharing!
